Project Case Study

Marfa Field Guide

Marfa Field Guide is an iOS location experience built to surface local landmarks, creative spaces, and scenic paths with context-aware recommendations.

The case study focused on performance in remote areas, including asset caching, offline-first map behavior, and navigation flows designed for low-connectivity travel.
